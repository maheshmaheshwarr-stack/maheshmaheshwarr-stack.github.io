About Me

I'm Mahesh Ramanathan, a PeopleSoft FSCM Subject Matter Expert with over 20 years of experience in enterprise software solutions.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with industry leaders including Oracle, Wipro, and Rimini Street. My expertise spans implementation, upgrade, and support projects across the PeopleSoft Financial Supply Chain Management suite.

Today, I'm focused on the exciting intersection of traditional ERP systems and modern AI technologies. I'm passionate about exploring how AI can modernize and enhance enterprise resource planning systems, making them more intelligent, efficient, and user-friendly. Beyond ERP modernization, I'm building tools that help people navigate information more effectively, applying AI to solve real-world problems in innovative ways.
